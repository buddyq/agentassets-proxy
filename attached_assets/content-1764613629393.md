# Buddy Quaid

737-500-1234

## Description

Tucked away in Eanes ISD’s coveted Westwood neighborhood – this mid-century modern home is a rare combination of natural privacy & neighborhood charm. Built in 1960 and beautifully updated in 2015, this authentic timepiece was designed to bring the outdoors…IN. Renovations include the open concept gourmet kitchen, luxurious primary suite and guest bedrooms. Perfect for dining al fresco with Fleetwood doors that graciously open onto a large deck with outdoor grill overlooking the private greenbelt and wet-weather creek. Mid-century features such as the of open floor plan living, vaulted ceilings and natural stone elements create a vibe that is effortlessly boho chic. Not included in the square footage is a separate HVAC’ed workshop/studio that is ready for your next creative venture!

Located in the highly sought-after Eanes school district and feeds to Eanes Elementary, Hill Country Middle School & Westlake High School. LOW TAX RATE at 1.68% and less than ½ mile to local shops, restaurants such as Sway and Texas Honey Ham, Breed Hardware, Barton Springs Nursery, fitness gyms, yoga and so much more. Come enjoy the best that Westlake has to offer at 410 Brookhaven Trail!

**Features Include:**

- Fleetwood Doors and Windows
- Jenn-Air Range
- Large Kitchen Island
- Hardwood, Tile and Concrete Floors
- Primary Suite with Private Access to Deck
- Separate Studio/Workshop
- 3 Living Areas
- 2 Wood-Burning Fireplaces (1 indoor / 1 outdoor)
- Backs to Greenbelt and Wet Weather Creek
